Key Findings

  • Good traffic, but no funnel structure to convert it at scale
  • Previous agency had maxed out bottom-funnel with no fresh acquisition strategy
  • 10x ROAS was proven at small spend (₹2.6L) but nobody had tried to scale it

Our Strategy

  • Doubled ad spend over 3 months while continuously optimising for efficiency
  • Full-funnel creative layering: brand films, product USPs, dynamic retargeting
  • Complete campaign restructuring + audience & geo refinement from ground up
  • Instagram placement campaigns to unlock a channel that wasn't being used

Achievements

  • Sustained 9x+ ROAS at 2x the original ad spend
  • ₹2.5 Cr in Year 1, ₹1.7 Cr in Year 2. Over ₹4 Cr total.
  • BAU ROAS improved from 1.8x to 3.5x
  • 3x Instagram revenue. Stronger traffic quality across the board.

Key Findings

  • Wrong audiences being targeted — luxury buyer segments hadn't been built
  • Existing campaigns underperforming with ROAS well below brand's true potential
  • No full-funnel structure to handle high spend (₹10–15L/month) profitably

Our Strategy

  • Deployed ₹10–15L/month in precision-targeted Meta & Google campaigns
  • Built separate acquisition and retargeting funnels for luxury buyer segments
  • Creative-led approach: aspirational visuals matched to high-intent audiences

Achievements

  • ~₹70L/month in revenue, sustained across all 4 months
  • ₹3–4 Cr total revenue on ₹50–60L ad spend
  • ~5x ROAS maintained despite aggressive budget scaling
  • Repeatable, scalable paid acquisition playbook established
